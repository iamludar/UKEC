Overview

BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT MANAGER | ref.MF155. An exciting new opportunity has arisen to join our client as a local Business Development Manager in their Douglas office. Our client are experts in UK immigration and investment, with a proven track record of assisting individuals and their families in achieving permanent residency through investment into high-growth UK companies. This role will identify and support innovative, emerging, and established IOM companies that are looking to raise capital for growth and development.

‍

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:

Working closely with their Dubai office, which are responsible for attracting high net-worth investors from around the globe; you would be responsible for promoting global investment opportunities to IOM and UK companies seeking investment for growth. You will be instrumental in driving sales, expanding customer base, and increasing revenue.

‍

SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:

You will be a dynamic and motivated person with at least 3 years of successful experience in B2B sales. The right candidate must prove they have an established history of building a successful sales territory within a service and support sales environment, ideally within people-oriented fields (e.g., Finance, insurance, property, immigration, etc.). with the ability to lead, influence, and deliver results effectively. To discuss this role further or to apply, email your CV to jobs@mckenziefox.com. You can call us on 01624 698900 quoting reference MF155. Apply: https://mckenziefox.com/jobs/business-development-manager-douglas-isle-of-man/1617-1/
