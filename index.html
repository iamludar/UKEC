The London Institute of Business and Technology (LIBT) joined business leaders, members, and dignitaries from across the island on Friday evening as the Isle of Man Chamber of Commerce marked a truly special milestone — its Annual Gala Dinner, held this year in celebration of the Chamber's 70th anniversary at the historic Peel Cathedral.

On 5 December 2025, the Isle of Man Chamber of Commerce hosted its Christmas Party at Launchpad in Douglas. The evening brought together members from across the local business community.
