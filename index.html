What UKEC does

A regional bridge between applicants, partners, organisations, and LIBT.

This homepage is intentionally focused on UKEC's role rather than repeating the full LIBT website. Use it as a clear starting point for regional enquiries and onward course exploration.

01

Course matching

Help learners compare diploma, top-up, postgraduate, and professional development routes based on goals and background.

02

Application guidance

Explain requirements, collect initial documents, and help applicants prepare a cleaner submission.

03

Partner support

Provide a single regional point of contact for Asia-Pacific education partners and referral channels.

04

Student communication

Answer practical questions before handoff to the relevant admissions or academic team.

05

Corporate training

Coordinate training and business service enquiries for organisations looking at professional programmes.

06

Local market insight

Support LIBT-facing conversations with regional context across learner needs, partner expectations, and delivery preferences.
