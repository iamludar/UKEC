The Centre for Learning Innovation & Knowledge Enhancement (LIKE)

The Centre for Learning Innovation and Knowledge Enhancement (LIKE) at the United Kingdom Education Centre (UKEC) focuses on advancing research and practice in digital learning, instructional design, and technology-enhanced education. The Centre brings together researchers, educators, and industry professionals to examine and develop effective models for learning in technology-mediated environments.

LIKE supports the development of evidence-based methodologies, emerging learning technologies, and frameworks for lifelong learning. Its work addresses the changing needs of learners and institutions, with an emphasis on research-driven solutions for business and technology education.
‍
Our Mission
‍The Centre’s mission is to lead in the development of research-informed approaches to instructional design and learning innovation. It aims to support the creation and dissemination of digital learning models, cultivate expertise in learning science, and contribute to professional communities through applied research and collaborative projects.

Call for Papers: Business Research Review (BRR)

The Centre publishes the Business Research Review (BRR), a peer-reviewed, open-access academic journal focusing on qualitative research at the intersection of business, technology, innovation, and entrepreneurship.

We invite researchers, academics, and practitioners to submit original papers for the inaugural issue of the 2025 volume. Submissions should align with the journal’s scope and adhere to the provided author guidelines. BRR aims to publish work that explores how technological advancement is influencing business practices, strategy, and organizational change. Learn more here.
